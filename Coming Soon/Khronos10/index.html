Get To Know Us.

Until now, it was nearly impossible for the 15 million self-employed, contract, and "gig economy" workers to find quality, short-term disability insurance plans.

America's Savings Crisis

Only 48 percent of Americans can cover three months of living costs if they are unable to work. For adults between the ages of 35 and 65, one in three are at risk of a disability that will keep them away from work for more than 90 days. Low savings combined with disability is disastrous for most Americans.

Why Disability Insurance?

Disability insurance provides enormous dividends by insuring people's income and securing financial and physical well-being. Although disability insurance is life changing and hedges against tremendous financial burden, at least 51 million working adults in the United States lack adequate disability insurance coverage.
